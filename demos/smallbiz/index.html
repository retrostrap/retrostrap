Benvenuti!

Welcome, and thanks for stopping by our corner of the web! Since 1987 the Esposito family makes pizza the slow way: good dough, one whole day of patience, and one very hot oven. Now we are also on the World Wide Web, grazie to our nephew Marco, who built this page over one loud weekend.

Look at the menu, find our door, come eat. We saved you a chair.

NEW! We now accept card payments!
