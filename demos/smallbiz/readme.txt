PIZZERIA BELLA NAPOLI 2000 -- readme.txt
=========================================

Ciao! Marco here. I am the nephew. I built this web page for my zia and zio
over one very loud weekend, and Nonna supervised from the kitchen with a
wooden spoon.

WHAT IS ON THE WEB PAGE
  index.html ...... welcome, today's special, opening hours, the oven
  menu.html ....... the whole menu, prices in euro, no pineapple
  find-us.html .... how to find our door (there is a little map)
  contact.html .... write to us for a table of six or more

HOW IT IS BUILT
  Plain HTML, dressed with retrostrap (the files in ../../dist/). No
  database, no shopping cart, no computer of ours listening on the other
  end. The reservation form opens YOUR e-mail program -- old fashioned,
  like the pizza.

NOTES
  * The oven picture I drew myself, pixel by pixel. It took longer than the
    rest of the site. Worth it.
  * If a price on the web page ever disagrees with the chalkboard, believe
    the chalkboard, and please tell me.
  * Everyone in here is invented. Any resemblance to a real pizzeria is a
    compliment we would happily accept in the form of a margherita.

Grazie for visiting. Come hungry.
  -- Marco, and the whole Esposito family
