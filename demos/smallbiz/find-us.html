Find our door

We are on Via Roma 12, one minute from Piazza Vecchia. Look for the red awning and follow your nose. If you reach the river, you walked one street too far, but the view is nice, so no harm done.

[P] = the little car park. Free after 19:00.

Getting here

  • By foot: from Piazza Vecchia, take Via Roma north. We are the third door, red awning.
  • By bus: lines 4 and 9 stop at “Vecchia”. Two minutes on foot from there.
  • By car: park at [P] one block west, or on Via Roma after 19:00.
  • By boat: you cannot, but people ask, so: no.

The essentials

  • Via Roma 12, in the old quarter
  • Telephone in the office, ring for tables of six or more
  • Steps at the door, sorry; ring the bell and we lend a hand
