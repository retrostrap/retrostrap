Raising a happy blobby

Blobbies are easy. Keep them fed, keep them warm, and they will keep you company while the page loads.

Every day:

  • Feed it once. Click the blobby in the corner and watch it go nom.
  • Say hello. A blobby that gets looked at is a blobby that stays.
  • Let it nap. If you wander off, it dozes until you come back.

Quick tips:

  • Waffles are a treat, not a meal. One a day is plenty.
  • A cold blobby is a sad blobby. Park it near a warm modem.
  • Blobbies remember you between visits, so visit often.
