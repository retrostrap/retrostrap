~ * ~ welcome to Cosmic OS ~ * ~

this whole desktop is one web page. drag the windows by the titlebar, with the mouse, with touch, or with the arrow keys once one is focused. the buttons in each corner shade, maximise, and close; the taskbar and the desktop icons bring a closed window back and lift an open one to the front.

the bouncing logo is the screensaver. we are all still waiting for it to hit the corner.

the taskbar clock stopped at 11:59 on the last night of 1999. we are keeping it there.

-- the webmaster
