            BUTTON WORKS 1.0  ~  readme.txt
            ================================

what it is
  a little paint program for making the one thing every homepage needs:
  an 88 by 31 pixel button. type your words, pick your colours, press
  Download, and a PNG lands on your computer. that is the whole program.
  it never phones home. it cannot. there is nowhere to phone.

how to drive it
  DESIGN ..... your text (14 letters, the button is small), a font, a
               tile pattern (solid / checker / stars / stripes), and a
               tick-box for the 3D bevel edge.
  PALETTE .... all 216 web-safe colours, plus the named sixteen. the
               radio in DESIGN decides whether a colour you click becomes
               the text, the background, or the border. arrow keys walk
               the grid, Enter picks. hover a swatch to read its hex.
  PREVIEW .... your button at 4x zoom and at true size, six starter
               buttons you can click to load, and the Download button.

presets
  classic navy . hot lava . terminal . bubblegum . roadworks . midnight
  they live in app.js (and, for the curious, in presets.json). every
  colour in them is web-safe or one of the named sixteen, on purpose.

coming eventually
  animated GIF export, a two-frame sparkle. not in 1.0. it is 1999 and
  patience is a bandwidth strategy.

  made with retrostrap. the demos hub's own buttons were made right here.
  now go make yours, and join a ring. -- the button works
