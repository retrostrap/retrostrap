About Button Works

Button Works 1.0, a retrostrap demonstration program. No server, no upload: your button is drawn on your own computer, like everything used to be.

Every homepage deserves a button. 88 by 31 pixels of pure self-expression. Pick your colours, all 216 lawful ones, plus the named sixteen, type your text, download your PNG, put it on your page, join a ring.

Animated GIF export (a two-frame sparkle) is coming eventually. It is 1999; patience is a virtue and a bandwidth strategy.
