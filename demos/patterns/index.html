The kugeln

Little shiny spheres that trail your pointer, a whole rainbow of them. Add the toybox script and one attribute:

<body data-rs-widgets="kugeln"
      data-rs-kugeln-colors="rainbow"
      data-rs-kugeln-mode="trail">

Switch mode to orbit for a ring that circles the cursor, or set colors to one of red, gold, green, cyan, blue, purple, pink, silver. They rest under reduced motion and never spawn on a touch screen, so the page is exactly as usable without them.

The wallpaper drawer

Each of these is a background you drop on any element with one rs-tile-* class. Tile the page, then float an opaque rs-page sheet on top: the centred-table-on-a-starfield anatomy the era ran on. Every tile stays inside the 224 legal colours and repeats seamlessly.
