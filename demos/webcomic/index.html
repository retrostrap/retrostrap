The rant

Cables, cables, everywhere

Monday, 9 July 2001 · posted by Marty, your cartoonist

Today's strip is barely fiction. I lost the connection three times drawing it, and twice it was me who tripped over the cord.

People keep asking why the comic updates at such odd hours. This is why. The internet in this house runs on one shared phone line and a very fragile treaty. When the modem starts to sing, everyone holds their breath.

Thank you for reading, whenever this finally loaded for you. If it made you smile, the vote button down the page is the whole payment plan. Back Thursday, line permitting.

- Marty
