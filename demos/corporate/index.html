About Nimbus Systems

Founded in 1991, Nimbus Systems Incorporated is a leading provider of enterprise middleware, managed infrastructure, and professional services. From our headquarters in Riverton, California, we help more than 1,400 organizations keep their systems talking to one another, around the clock and around the world.

Our solutions are built on open standards and proven engineering. We measure success the way our customers do: in uptime, in throughput, and in results delivered on time and within budget.

Our mission

To empower the world's enterprises with reliable, scalable infrastructure, and to deliver lasting value to our shareholders, our customers, and our people.

Products

Three product families, one platform. Each is sold on its own or as part of an integrated Nimbus deployment.

NimbusServe

Our flagship application server. Deploys your business logic across commodity hardware and scales from a single node to a full data-center cluster.

NimbusLink

Enterprise messaging and integration middleware. Connects mainframe, midrange, and web systems with guaranteed, once-only delivery.

NimbusVault

Managed backup and archival for mission-critical data. Nightly snapshots, off-site replication, and audited recovery you can rehearse.

Services

Software is only half the job. Our professional services group stays with you from the first review to the last cut-over.

Consulting

Architecture reviews, capacity planning, and migration strategy from our senior engineers.

Systems integration

We connect what you already own to what you buy next, on schedule and on budget.

Managed support

Round-the-clock coverage, a named account engineer, and a service-level agreement in writing.

Training

Instructor-led and self-paced courses at our campus or yours; certification available.
