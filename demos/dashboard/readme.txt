what this is
  the status board we keep on the monitor bolted to the server-room
  wall. green on black because that is the only colour the old CRT
  still shows without warming up for ten minutes. it refreshes every
  five seconds by itself. do not stand too close, the phosphor stares
  back.

reading it
  FLEET ......... every host, its job, its CPU, and one word of status.
                 the word is the truth. OK, WARN, CRIT. if it says CRIT
                 it is CRIT even if the glow looks fine to you.
  MODEM POOL .... how full the dial-up banks are. busy signal = full.
  BANDWIDTH ..... the uplink graph, drawn in # because plotting costs
                 money and we spent it on modems.

the buttons
  [poll now] .... roll the numbers this instant, do not wait five seconds.
  [freeze] ...... stop the auto-refresh (for photographs, or for peace).
                 add ?rs-freeze=1 to the address to boot up frozen.

about mimas
  mimas is the backup server. mimas is at 99%. mimas has been at 99%
  since the tape drive learned to fear. we have a plan. the plan is Iris.

Y2K
  yes we know. the freeze is on. no changes until Jan 03. the fax
  machine is the last 6% and the fax machine is not our friend.

  built with retrostrap. no cookies, no trackers, no uplink to anywhere
  but the rack. -- the night shift
