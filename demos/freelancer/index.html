How it works

Three steps, start to finish. No jargon, no runaround.

From idea to online in three steps

  1. You tell me your idea. Fill in the quote form below, or just e-mail me. Tell me what your business is and what you'd like the site to do. There's no charge to ask, and I answer the same day.
  2. I build a mock-up. I sketch a home page and send it over for you to poke at. We go back and forth, colours, words, that animated logo, until it's right. Only then do I build out the rest.
  3. I put it on the web. When you're happy, I upload the whole thing, test every link and form, hand you the files, and show you how to make small edits yourself. You're live, and you can find your own site on the internet. Magic.
