How to connect

Pull down the console with the tilde key ~, paste the line, hit enter:

C:\> vortex console
connect 203.0.113.42:27015; password frag

That address is a documentation stand-in, not a live box, this is a demo page. The password is rotated weekly; ask an officer if it bounces you.

House rules

  • No cheats, no scripts, no macros. Ever. One strike, gone.
  • Admins have the last word. Argue after the round, not during.
  • Mic spam and slur-slinging get you kicked, then banned.
  • Scrims and matches take the server, watch the message of the day.
  • Lost? Down a round? Say gg and mean it.

  • No cheating, no scripts. Non-negotiable, and we do check demos.
  • Own a working mic and use it, calls win rounds.
  • Make at least two scrim nights a week. We practise, we don't just pub.
