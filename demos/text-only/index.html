About this page

Hi, I'm Sandra. I'm fourteen and I like the stars. This is the plain version of my homepage. I made it because my friend Priya uses a screen reader and my cousin browses on a text terminal, and everyone deserves the good stuff. It is exactly the same words as the fancy page, just without the cat and the snow.

People call pages like this "Lynx-friendly," and there is a little "Bobby Approved" badge you can earn. I just call it good manners: black on white, big links, nothing that needs a mouse.
