Hi, I'm Sam. Welcome to my corner of the web, such as it is. I registered the space with big plans, a library book on HTML, and one free weekend. The weekend ended; the plans did not. Most of the site is still in boxes, but the door is open and the kettle is on, so please look around at the parts that exist.

Sam's note: if a link takes you nowhere, that is not a bug, that is the roadmap. ~thanks for your patience~

So, what happened?

A fair question. Here is the honest timeline. I built the homepage first, because the homepage is the fun part, and left the actual pages for "the weekend." Then the weekend got busy, and the one after that, and it turns out learning to align things without tables is its own kind of home renovation.

The scanner ate a photo and has been sulking since. The guestbook, by some miracle, works perfectly. So that is where things stand: one working homepage, one working guestbook, and a great many good intentions with borders around them.

Good news: nothing here is broken on purpose. It's just early. Bookmark the place and the rest will fill in.
