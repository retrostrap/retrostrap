THE NEBULA SHRINE -- readme.txt
================================

Greetings, cadet. NovaKnight97 here, webmaster of the number one (and only?)
fan shrine for STAR QUEST 3D, the greatest space sim ever pressed to CD-ROM.
Meteorhead Software gave us 40 missions and 9 ships. This whole site is my
thank-you letter.

WHAT IS ON THE SHRINE
  index.html ..... the front page: news, jukebox, the sequel banner, awards
  story.html ..... the whole plot, with the ENDING behind a spoiler tag
  ships.html ..... all 9 flyable ships, factory stats, targeting reticle on
  cheats.html .... the public cheat table (BIGCHEESE, SQHULL9, and friends)
  secret.html .... not in any menu. you know the code. everyone knows the code.

HOW IT IS BUILT
  Plain HTML, dressed with retrostrap (the files in ../../dist/). No frames
  tag was harmed -- the "frameset" look is CSS, so the back button tells the
  truth and bookmarks work. The starfield, the cursor comet, and the secret-code
  easter egg are retrostrap widgets. Nothing here phones home.

THE JUKEBOX
  Three chiptune loops, reused across six track titles, exactly like a real
  1998 CD trying to fit under 650 MB. Nothing plays until you click a track --
  that is the law, and also just good manners.

NOTES
  * Star Quest 3D, Meteorhead Software, and every ship, code and award on this
    page are INVENTED. This is a tribute to a game that never existed. Any
    resemblance to a real space sim is a compliment I would accept in the form
    of a soundtrack CD.
  * The awards and the sequel banner I drew myself, pixel by pixel.
  * Best viewed in any browser, at any size, at warp speed.

Fly safe out there.
  -- NovaKnight97
