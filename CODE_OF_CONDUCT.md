# Code of conduct

Retrostrap is a friendly corner of the web and we intend to keep it that way.
The short version: behave like it's a well-moderated 1998 forum.

1. Be kind. Critique code and pixels, never people.
2. Assume good faith. Most "wrong" is just "new here".
3. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination. Zero tolerance.
4. No flamewars. Disagree once, with arguments, then let it rest.
5. No spam, no unsolicited advertising, no drive-by self-promo.
6. Keep it worksafe, 1999 school computer lab rules apply.
7. English and German are both welcome (Der Stammtisch exists for a reason);
   be patient with non-native speakers, most of us are.
8. The maintainers moderate: we may edit, hide, or remove content and ban
   repeat offenders. We explain once, then we act.

Report problems privately to conduct@retrostrap.dev or via GitHub's report
feature. Adapted from our
Netiquette charter ([docs/11](docs/11-community-forum.md)); applies to issues,
PRs, the Boards, and any space we run.
